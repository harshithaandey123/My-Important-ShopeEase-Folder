# ShopEase Copilot

## AI-Powered Multilingual Conversational Shopping Assistant

### Microsoft Build AI Hackathon 2026

ShopEase Copilot is an AI-powered multilingual shopping assistant that transforms online shopping into a natural conversation. Instead of navigating multiple screens, filters, and product pages, users can simply ask for products, compare options, manage their cart, and complete purchases using conversational commands.

Our vision is to make online shopping accessible, intuitive, and inclusive for the next generation of digital users, especially regional-language speakers and first-time online shoppers.

---

## Problem Statement

Current e-commerce platforms create several challenges for users:

* Product overload makes decision-making difficult.
* Searching, filtering, and comparing products is time-consuming.
* Most platforms are optimized primarily for English-speaking users.
* Shopping journeys involve multiple screens and manual interactions.
* Accessibility remains limited for voice-first and regional-language users.

These challenges often result in lower engagement, cart abandonment, reduced customer satisfaction, and lost revenue for businesses.

---

## Our Solution

ShopEase Copilot enables users to shop through natural conversations.

Users can simply interact with the assistant using commands such as:

* Best earbuds under ₹1500
* Compare the first and second products
* Add the second product to cart
* Show my cart
* Proceed to checkout

The assistant understands user intent, provides product recommendations, performs comparisons, manages cart operations, and supports multilingual interactions.

Unlike traditional shopping assistants that only provide information, ShopEase Copilot acts as an intelligent shopping companion capable of understanding requests and performing shopping actions conversationally.

---

## Key Innovations

### Multilingual Understanding

ShopEase Copilot can understand user requests in multiple languages, including English, Telugu, and Hindi.

Examples:

**English**
Show me the best earbuds

**Telugu**
నాకు మంచి ఇయర్ బడ్స్ చూపించు

**Hindi**
सबसे अच्छे ईयरबड्स दिखाओ

This helps regional-language users interact naturally with online shopping platforms.

### Conversational Shopping Actions

Most existing shopping assistants provide recommendations but require users to manually navigate product pages and carts.

ShopEase Copilot enables users to perform actions directly through conversation, including:

* Product search
* Product comparison
* Cart management
* Checkout assistance

### Voice-Enabled Commerce

The solution is designed to support voice-based shopping experiences through speech recognition and speech synthesis technologies, enabling hands-free shopping workflows.
